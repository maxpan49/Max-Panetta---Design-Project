Soccer, also known as football in Europe, is a team sport played with eleven players on two different teams. The main goal of the game is to work and strategize with your team to to score more goals than your opponent. It is rare to see a team score more than four goals, the average being anything lower than that. Soccer is played on a rectangular pitch that measures 115 yards where there is a goal at each end to score the point by kicking the ball into the net before ninety minutes and stoppage time, which varies by the amount of minutes wasted because of stops. There is also stoppage time at 45 minutes, which is the half. If the game is still tied but it's important and there needs to be a winner to move on, it goes up to 120 minutes. If there is still no goal, the players take penalties 12 yards away from the goal line to determine the winner. Soccer is played in over 200 countries. According to FIFA, there are over 265 million professional soccer players in the world and around 3.5 billion people watch the sport, easily making it the most popular sport in the world.
